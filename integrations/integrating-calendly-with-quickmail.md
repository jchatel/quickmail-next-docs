# Integrating Calendly with QuickMail

**In this article:**

- What is Calendly integration for?

- How to use Calendly in QuickMail?

- How to track the number of bookings?

## What Is Calendly Integration For?

Calendly integration allows you to track leads who have booked through your Calendly link.

## How to Use Calendly in QuickMail?

Paste your Calendly link into an email step and add it as a hyperlink.

**Note:** The email address associated with the Calendly link must be added as an email account in QuickMail. Otherwise, bookings will not be detected.

![screenshot](../images/000_file-knu7u3MX90.png)

**Note:** When a lead books a call from your Calendly link, their status in the campaign will be marked as "Booked" and follow-up emails will stop being sent to them.

## How to Track the Number of Bookings?

The total number of bookings can be found in the Dashboard. To see bookings for a specific period, use the **Booked** filter in the analytics.
