# FAQ: Does QuickMail provide pre-warmed domains?

**

No, we don’t provide pre-warmed domains.

**

However, you can buy new but ready-to-use Gmail inboxes directly in QuickMail in just a few clicks. We set them up for you, including DNS records, and automatically connect them to your QuickMail account.

These inboxes come with S****tealth mode**, which helps emails behave like normal Gmail sending. Because of this, there’s no warm-up required and you can start sending right away at low volume.

The cost is **$40 per 10 email accounts**, and it requires an active QuickMail subscription.
