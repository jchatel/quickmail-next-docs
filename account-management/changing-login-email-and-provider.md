# Changing Your Login Email or Login Provider

**In this article:**

- How to change my email for logging in?

- How to change my login provider?

## How to Change My Email for Logging In?

To change your login email, log in to QuickMail using your current email, then follow these steps:

Go to **Team** → **+ Member** → **Generate Invite Link** → copy the invite link.

Open the invite link in an incognito window and accept the invite using your new email address.

## How to Change My Login Provider?

Only QuickMail support can change your login provider. Contact them at support@quickmail.io to request the change.

Once you reach out, they will send you a 2FA code. Provide that code to them for security verification purposes.
