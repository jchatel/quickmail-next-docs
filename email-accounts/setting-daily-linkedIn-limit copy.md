# Setting Daily LinkedIn Limits

**In this article:**
Why limit daily LinkedIn sends?
How Does the Daily LinkedIn Limit Work?
How to Set Up the Daily LinkedIn Limit?

## Why Limit Daily LinkedIn Sends?

The daily sending limit allows you to control how many actions are sent from your LinkedIn account each day. This helps reduce the risk of your LinkedIn account being restricted due to unusual activity.

## How Does the Daily LinkedIn Limit Work?

The daily sending limit sets the maximum number of profile views, connection requests, LinkedIn messages, LinkedIn voice messages, and LinkedIn InMails that can be sent from your LinkedIn account each day.

## How to Set Up the Daily LinkedIn Limit?

Go to LinkedIn → select the LinkedIn account you want to configure → Sending → scroll down → set your preferred daily limits for profile views, connection requests, messages, voice messages, and InMails.

**Note:** You can also increase the delay between sending actions to make your LinkedIn activity appear more natural and less automated, helping reduce the risk of account restrictions.
