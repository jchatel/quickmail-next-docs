# Setting a Campaign to Private

**In this article:**

- How to set a campaign to private

**Note:** By default, the campaign owner is the user who created the campaign.

Setting a campaign to private restricts access so that only the campaign owner can view and manage it. This is useful when you want to prevent other team members from editing or managing your campaigns.

## How to Set a Campaign to Private

### Option 1: From Campaign Settings

Open the campaign → click **Settings**.

![screenshot](../images/000_file-OijeGhnpO0.png)

Go to **Sharing Settings** → change from **Shared** to **Private**.

![screenshot](../images/001_file-IKISBppUin.png)

### Option 2: During Initial Campaign Setup

You can also set a campaign to private when creating it. Select **Only Me** during the setup process.

![screenshot](../images/002_file-a9NAmqiVOW.png)

**Note:** Users who are not the campaign owner cannot view or make changes to the campaign, even if they are an admin.
