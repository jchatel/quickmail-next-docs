# FAQ: Agency accounts

Q: How many team members can I add? You can add as many team members as needed at** no additional cost.**
For step-by-step guides on how to add team members, check out these pages:
- For Teams: Adding Team Members
- For Agencies: Team Members

Q: Can I be keep multiple workspaces under the agency plan, while only paying for one workspace at a time?
It's not possible to do that and we can't make exceptions.
Agency billing is priced for total number of workspaces, it's not possible to keep one inactive and another one active.
