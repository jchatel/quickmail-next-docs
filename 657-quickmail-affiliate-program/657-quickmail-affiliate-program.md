# QuickMail Affiliate Program

**

We appreciate your support in sharing QuickMail with others! This article outlines how our commission structure works and how you can earn rewards when you refer new customers.

**

## How to Sign Up?

To sign up, go to [https://affiliates.quickmail.com/login](https://affiliates.quickmail.com/login). Please note that not all applications are accepted, you’ll receive an email confirming whether your application has been approved.

## Commission Structure

Our commissions are designed to reward you fairly and transparently. All commissions are automatically approved 30 days after they are generated.

Important**:

- A minimum of $100 in commission is required before you can request a payout

- The email you used to sign up as an affiliate must be associated with a Wise account for us to process payouts
