# Why start a trial?

Trial accounts allow you to explore QuickMail and see if it matches your needs.

Additionally, not all cold email software performs the same in terms of deliverability, automation, and integrations. Using trial accounts helps you compare QuickMail with other tools and make an informed decision.

# How to start a trial?

To get started, please go to https://next.quickmail.com/signup.

If you're doing outreach for yourself, click Single account and Agency if you're doing outreach for your clients.

# What's included in the trial?

During the trial, you can access all features but you're limited to:

- Import up to 100 leads

- Send up to 100 emails daily

- Add unlimited emails for sending

# Troubleshooting

## Why am I being asked for a card?

We only require a card to prevent spammers from abusing the system.

We won't automatically charge you after the trial ends.

## Where do I cancel the trial?

No need to cancel trial accounts.

Just let it expire and I can assure you that you won't get charged after the trial expires.

We don't automatically charge customers without their permission.

## Why can I only add 100 leads and send 100 emails daily?

Trial accounts are only meant to test if features fit your needs.

So running a campaign in full swing is not possible.

To run a campaign, please upgrade your plan from the billing page.
