# FAQ: What are your available CRM integrations?

Source: https://help.quickmail.com/article/500-faq-what-are-your-available-crm-integrations

We have an integration with HubSpot and PipeDrive.

Our HubSpot and PipeDrive integration allows you to export, import, automatically sync tags and attributes, and see your prospect's campaign activities (opens, clicks, replies, bounces, and unsubscribe) in HubSpot. Here's a quick breakdown of how the two-way sync works:

- [Integrating HubSpot and QuickMail](https://help.quickmail.com/article/566-new-ui-hubspot-integration)
- [Integrating PipeDrive with QuickMail](https://help.quickmail.com/article/600-new-ui-pipedrive-integration)

If you need more than this, we have a Zapier integration and Webhooks.

Zapier allows you to use QuickMail with tools we don't have a native integration with, as long as the tool has Zapier integration too.

Some trigger events that we have on Zapier are opens, clicks, replies, unsubscribes, bounces, and tags applied to name some.

As for the actions, you can update prospects and add them either to a campaign or bucket.

Here's a detailed guide on [integrating Zapier](https://help.quickmail.com/article/563-new-ui-zapier-integration) in QuickMail.
