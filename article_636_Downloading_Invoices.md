# Downloading Invoices

[__](javascript:window.print\(\) "Print this article")

**Note:** Invoices can only be downloaded by account admins. Team members will have error downloading invoices

## **For Agencies:**

The invoices can be downloaded in the agency dashboard.

To do that, go to the agency dashboard → Billing → Click "See all" under Next Invoice

![](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/57abccd7c697910783243d9b/images/679a1a7b4bfa3334f17de2ab/file-ju9o2uhAtG.png)

After that, go to "Past" tab → Download invoices

![](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/57abccd7c697910783243d9b/images/679a1a97416fc46b5dc4147f/file-BMYX49QCL0.png)

  

## **For Teams:**

The invoices must be downloaded in the workspace-level.

To do that, go to workspace Settings → Billing/Plan → Click "See all" under Next Invoice

![](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/57abccd7c697910783243d9b/images/6798eb0a41983028e6dec6ce/file-a0UYpXbeFy.png)

After that, go to "Past" tab → Download invoices

![](//d33v4339jhl8k0.cloudfront.net/docs/assets/57abccd7c697910783243d9b/images/6798eafb41983028e6dec6cd/file-EAHtn8Nz4N.png)
