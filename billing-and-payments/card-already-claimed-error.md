# Resolving 'Card is Already Claimed' Error 

A payment card can only be added to one QuickMail account at a time. This restriction helps prevent abuse and protects the platform from spam.

If you need to use the same payment card on two different QuickMail accounts, please contact our support team by clicking the AI chatbot in the lower-right corner of your QuickMail account, then selecting "Escalate to a Human."

Our team can remove the restriction, allowing you to use the same card on the additional account.
