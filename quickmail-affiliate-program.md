# QuickMail Affiliate Program

We appreciate your support in sharing QuickMail with others! This article outlines how our commission structure works and how you can earn rewards when you refer new customers.

## How to Sign Up?

To sign up, go to [https://affiliates.quickmail.com/login](https://affiliates.quickmail.com/login). Please note that not all applications are accepted, you’ll receive an email confirming whether your application has been approved.

## Commission Structure

Our commissions are designed to reward you fairly and transparently. All commissions are automatically approved 30 days after they are generated.

**Important**:

- A minimum of $100 in commission is required before you can request a payout

- The email you used to sign up as an affiliate must be associated with a Wise account for us to process payouts

|---------------------|----------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------|
| Tier 1              | 5%             | Less than 10             | None                        |
| Tier 2              | 10%            | 10–19                    | None                        |
| Tier 3              | 15%            | 20–29                    | None                        |

| Tier 4 - High Price | 20%            | More than 29             | $80/mo or more per customer |


## Frequently Asked Questions
**Can a referral be added after an account has already been created?**

No. Referrals must be tracked and attributed when the account is created. If the account was not created through your affiliate referral link, it cannot be manually added to your affiliate account later.

**Why wasn't my referral credited?**

QuickMail uses Tolt to track affiliate referrals. If a customer signs up directly instead of using your referral link, Tolt cannot attribute the account to you, and the referral will not be credited.

**The customer hasn't activated or paid for their account yet. Can the referral still be added?**

No. The referral must be associated with the account at the time of signup. The account's activation or payment status does not affect referral attribution.

**Can QuickMail manually assign a referral to my affiliate account?**

No. Since referrals are tracked automatically through Tolt, QuickMail cannot manually assign or modify referral attribution after an account has been created.

**Is there any workaround if a customer forgot to use my referral link?**

Yes. The customer can create a new QuickMail account using your referral link. 

**Can I refer myself?**

No. Self-referrals are automatically invalidated by Tolt. The email address used to create the referred account must not be associated with your affiliate account.
