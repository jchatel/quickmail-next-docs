# Cloning a Campaign

Cloning a campaign is a smart time-saver, allowing you to replicate all the steps and settings of an existing campaign. This eliminates the need to build everything from scratch, so you can quickly launch a new campaign with the same structure and settings.

## What elements will be copied when cloning a campaign?

**Elements That Will Be Copied:**

- All steps within the campaign

- Assigned email accounts

- Send times

- All campaign settings, except those listed below

**Elements That Will NOT Be Copied:**

- Leads added

- Assigned LinkedIn account

- Sub-campaigns

- "Add to Library" setting

- "Confirm unsubscribe" setting

## How to clone a campaign?

It's easy! Go to the campaign you'd like to copy → Menu (three vertical dots) → Clone campaign

After cloning the campaign, you'll be directed to the new cloned campaign
