# FAQ: Does QuickMail have its own auto-warmer?

Yes — QuickMail has its own built-in auto-warmer, but it’s currently only available for email accounts purchased through QuickMail.
To know if you have auto-warmer available, go to Emails > click the email > check if there's an auto-warmer tab.

If there's none, it means your email is not bought from us.

It will soon be available for other email accounts as well.

In the meantime, if you’re using email accounts from outside QuickMail, you can use [Mailflow’s](https://www.mailflow.io/) (an autowarmer tool we have direct integration with) auto-warming feature for free.

Once you’ve created a Mailflow account and have an active QuickMail subscription, click on the AI chatbot located in the lower-right corner of your QuickMail account, then click 'Escalate to a Human', and we’ll switch your Mailflow subscription to the free plan with increased warm-up limits.
