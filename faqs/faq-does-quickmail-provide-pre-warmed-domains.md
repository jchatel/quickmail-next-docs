# FAQ: Does QuickMail Provide Pre-Warmed Domains?

No, QuickMail does not provide pre-warmed domains.

However, you can buy new, ready-to-use Gmail accounts directly in QuickMail in just a few clicks. QuickMail sets them up for you, including DNS records, and automatically connects them to your account.

These email accounts come with **Stealth Mode**, which helps emails behave like normal Gmail sending. Because of this, no warm-up is required and you can start sending at low volume right away.

The cost is **$40 per 10 email accounts**, and an active QuickMail subscription is required.
