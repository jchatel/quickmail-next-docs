# FAQ: What’s included in my QuickMail trial?

When creating an account, it will automatically start on a **14-day trial** by default.

During the trial, users can:

- Send up to **100 emails per day**
- Add up to **100 leads in total**
- No limit on email senders and LinkedIn

If you need to send more emails or add more leads, a paid subscription is required.

At the end of the trial, you will **not be charged automatically**. We only charge with your permission, so there’s no need to manually cancel your subscription.
