# FAQ: Can I restore deleted campaigns or leads?

Unfortunately, no. Once a campaign or lead has been deleted, it cannot
be restored.

Deleted data is permanently removed from our system and cannot be
recovered by users or by the QuickMail Support team. This is by design
to protect customer privacy and ensure compliance with our data handling
and retention policies.

For this reason, we recommend confirming that a campaign or lead is no
longer needed before deleting it, as the action is irreversible.
